1.3 Lead Allocation Algorithm
Automated assignment based on:
                  * Round-robin allocation to logged-in team members

                  * Lead allocation to be done till 8hrs from login time. Last one hour to complete pending followups and tasks. 

                  * By Tags:

                     * B2B → B2B team only
                     * B2C → B2C team only
                     * Corporate → B2B team only
                     * Influencer/NRI/HNI/Reference → B2C team only

                        * Workload Distribution:
                        * Daily lead handling limit: 30 sales leads per person
                        * When someone reaches their limit, they are skipped in round-robin allocation

                           * Excess Workload Handling (extra leads):
                           * Leads remain in system unassigned initially
                           * Automated requirement-gathering message sent
                           * Responses are taken and fed into lead fields.
                           * Lead scoring runs automatically as per the updated data. 
                           * Qualified HOT leads are then automatically distributed to working team members.
                           * Criteria to allocate leads - Max leads to one who has minimum open leads in the system. 

                              * Alerts:
                              * Warning to TL/Manager when an agent reaches 90% workload of today’s leads + overall open count.
                              * Notifications for all qualified leads in Excess Workload bucket

                                 * Optional 
                                 * By City
                                 * By Segment

                                    * Team Structure:
                                    * B2C: 10 members (expanding to 15)
                                    * B2B: 5 members

1.11 Lead Scoring
0 - 10 Model
Final Lead Score Range
                                                                        * Hot: 7 - 10
                                                                        * Warm: 4 - 6
                                                                        * Cold: 0 - 3
                                                                        * Inactive: No response 
                                                                        * 48 hrs → Orange
                                                                        * 72 hrs → Red
1. Travel Date (Urgency Score: 0 to 3 points)
                                                                        * Duration from lead created date 
                                                                        * 3 points: Within 0 - 10 days.
                                                                        * 2 points: Within 10 - 30 days.
                                                                        * 1 point: Within 30 - 60 days.
                                                                        * 0 points: Within 60+ days.
2. Budget Fit (0 to 2 points)
                                                                        * Price of product chosen or budget mentioned in response 
                                                                        * 2 points: Customer’s budget is equal to or higher than the property price.
                                                                        * 1 point: The customer's budget is 10 - 20% lower than the property price.
                                                                        * 0 points: Customer’s budget is more than 20% lower than the property price.
3. Engagement Level (0 to 2 points) - 
                                                                        * Response rate via WhatsApp or calls.
                                                                        * 2 points: 90% +
                                                                        * 1 points: Up to 70% 
                                                                        * 0 point: Up to 50%
4. Call back requested (0 to 1 point)
                                                                        * Customer asked to call back by pressing IVR option
                                                                        * 1 point - Yes 
                                                                        * 0 points: No
5. Deal Size (0 to 2 point)
                                                                        * Traveler type mentioned 
                                                                        * 2 point: 50000+ 
                                                                        * 1 point: 20000 - 50000
                                                                        * 0 point: 0 - 20000
As per the total score Lead Categorisation will be -
                                                                        * Hot Leads (Score: 7 - 10)
                                                                        * Priority assignment to agents.

                                                                           * Warm Leads (Score: 4 - 6)
                                                                           * Standard follow-up workflow..

                                                                              * Cold Leads (Score: 0–3)
                                                                              * Auto Lost if still active and no response for 2 times 
Inactive Leads
                                                                              * 48 hours no response: Mark leads as Orange.

                                                                              * 72 hours no response: Mark lead as Red and notify Team Lead.
